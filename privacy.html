Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to BalanceCar, an iOS game developed for iPhone and iPad.

Developer Contact

Developer account and contact email: hengbozail@163.com.

Information We Store Locally

BalanceCar stores gameplay settings and progress on your device, including unlocked levels, best star ratings, completion state, and music preferences. This local game data is used only to provide the gameplay experience and is not an account system.

Advertising And Device Identifiers

BalanceCar may use third-party advertising SDKs to load and display ads. Where required by iOS, the app asks for permission before accessing the device advertising identifier for personalized advertising. If permission is denied, ads may still be shown in a non-personalized or limited form depending on the ad provider.

Network Access

The core game does not require an internet connection. Network access may be used by advertising services for ad loading, measurement, fraud prevention, and related ad delivery functions.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require user registration or a login.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, passwords, or payment information inside the game.
  • We do not provide in-app purchases, leaderboards, or cloud sync.

Children's Privacy

BalanceCar is designed as a general-audience game. The app does not knowingly request personal information from children. If you believe personal information has been provided through the app, contact us so we can review the issue.

Data Retention

Local progress and settings remain on your device until you delete the app or clear the app data. Advertising-related data, if any, is handled by the relevant ad providers according to their own policies.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes or when legal, platform, or advertising requirements change. Updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.
